What is RTA Next

A $2.4 billion investment in how Pima County moves.

RTA Next is the Regional Transportation Authority's plan to rebuild, expand, and modernize transportation infrastructure across Pima County through 2031. Every project is funded by the half-cent regional transportation tax voters approved in 2023.

This site is your direct window into what's being built, where, when, and why — no jargon, no press releases.

Business Assistance Plan

If construction is affecting access to your business, the RTA Next Business Assistance Program provides real, practical help — not just pamphlets.

🪧
Wayfinding signage
Directional signs installed at your request to redirect customers during closures.
🅿️
Parking coordination
Temporary parking arrangements for customers and delivery trucks while lots are displaced.
📞
Dedicated liaison
A named RTA contact assigned to your street segment — call or email directly.
